This is the official website for the Montana PM-2.5 Concentration Map project. We created a daily, PM-2.5 concentration map for Montana. Our next objective is to offer 3-day projections for those concentrations. This project is a joint partnership between Montana Tech and the University of Montana. We are funded by the Montana IDeA Network of Biomedical Research Excellence (INBRE).
